About

The Almost A Hero Playable Ad was an interactive advertisement built for the publisher "Tilting Point." Tilting Point wanted to showcase the key gameplay aspects of Almost A Hero, and provide a direct gameplay-based advertisement to the developers.

This is only one of the many interactive advertisements we've done with Tilting Point, and has been a hit with publishers & studios alike. The nature of the advertisement brings a new level of engagement for the viewers, and is much more exciting than the boring interstitial.

We took advantage of our HTML5 skills, and created everything in pure Javascript to ensure compatability with all ad networks. The ad was entirely under 1MB, with no external references. That includes all images and code.

Design

Design was done by the amazing people at Bee Square Games. All graphics, animations, and effects were created by them and we adapted them to fit the environment and size limits.

Development

Development was done with HTML5 and Pixi.JS. The game is built using Pixi.JS's rendering capabilities and is embeddable in any page or webview, as you can see here

Platforms

The game was developed for mobile (iOS & Android). Since it's built in HTML5, it's runnable in any web environment that supports Canvas. The game adapts to all screen sizes.

Distribution

This ad was distributed through AppLovin, InMobi, and Google Adwords ad networks. Each network needed its specific implementation, and we took care of that.

Cross Platform Mobile Development

A lot of projects require cross platform development. Whether it needs to be played on iOS, Android, Mac OSX, Windows, Xbox, or simply the web browser, we make it work.
