About

Pixel Pete is a 2D pixel art vertical casual game designed for Android and iOS. Built with Flash and ActionScript 3.0 using the Adobe AIR framework. Contains attractive pixel art graphics, and took approximately 4 weeks.

This was the second game built by the studio, and boasts beautiful graphics, a innovative weapon customization system led by a gyroscope-based rotating and interactive weapon. The game is an infinite runner at the core, with an array of unique, fun enemies to fight through.

Design

Design was done with Gimp and Photoshop. Animations were created using Gimp for hand-drawn spritesheets. Gimp & Photoshop are our go-to options for pixel art designs.

Development

Development was done with Flash, coded in ActionScript 3.0, and built for Android & iOS using Adobe Air. Adobe Flash has since become essentially obselete, but was one of the most popular out there.

Platforms

The game was developed with mobile (iOS & Android) in mind. It also runs on PC, Mac & Linux, using Flash's cross-platform capabilities and Adobe Air.

Distribution

Distributed on the Google Play Store. For distribution, we used ASO techniques, just to showcase our marketting skills. We provide ASO for all our projects, if it applies.

Monetization

We put together interstitial ads, video ads, and banner ads using Google AdMob.

Cross Platform Development

A lot of projects require cross platform development. Whether it needs to be played on iOS, Android, Mac OSX, Windows, Xbox, or simply the web browser, we make it work. Pixel Pete was built cross-platform for iOS, Android, Mac OSX, and Windows all with a single codebase. This was done using Adobe Flash with Adobe Air.

Design

For design we took advantage of our beautiful pixel art abilities. Using Gimp and Photoshop, we were able to create gorgeous graphics and effects with our heavily practiced skills and artistic eyes. For this game, we felt the pixel art style worked perfectly for our target audience. We also just love making it.
